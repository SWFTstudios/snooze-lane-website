If I'm one of the first 100 sign-ups, do I really get premium features and updates for free?

Absolutely! When Snooze Lane fully launches, you will receive an email containing a 100% off discount code. This will grant you access to our premium subscription, including all features and future updates, completely free of charge.

02

Is my location data safe and private?

Yes, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We only use your location data to ensure the app's functionality and never share or sell your data to third parties.

03

What happens if I miss my wake-up alert?

Snooze Lane is designed with multiple backup alerts to ensure you wake up when needed. Remember to keep your phone's volume up and avoid using 'Do Not Disturb' mode during your commute.

Is the app compatible with all smartphones?

How does the app calculate the estimated nap duration?

The estimated nap duration is calculated using real-time data, factoring in your current location, the distance to your destination, and the average travel speed.
