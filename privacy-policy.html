Our Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Snooze Lane. Your privacy is crucial to us, and this policy describes how we handle your information.

What Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We may collect your name, email address and phone number  when you sign up for our waitlist and/or when you interact with certain features on our app.
  • Location Data: Our app collects location data to ensure its core functionality of waking you up at the right destination. This data remains confidential.
  • Usage Data: We gather data on how you use our app to enhance its functionality and improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Our Service: We use your data to ensure Snooze Lane works efficiently, helping you nap worry-free during your commutes.
  • To Improve Our Service: Usage data helps us identify areas of improvement to ensure a seamless experience.
  • For Marketing Purposes: We might occasionally use your email to inform you of updates or offers related to Snooze Lane.

Who We Share Your Data With

‍We value your trust and assure you that we don't sell your data. We might share it with third-party service providers for the sole purpose of enhancing our app's functionality, always ensuring your privacy.

How We Protect Your Information

‍We employ top-notch security measures to safeguard your information, ensuring it remains confidential and secure.

Your Rights and Choices

‍You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us for any such requests.

Third-Party Services and Links

Changes to This Privacy Policy

‍We might occasionally update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any major changes will be communicated to you.
